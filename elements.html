Harry Potter Books

A complete list of the main series and companion reads.

Main series

Below are the seven canonical novels in the main Harry Potter series with short descriptions and suggested reading notes.

1. Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Introduction to Harry, Hogwarts, and the magical world. Great starter for young readers and first-time fans.

2. Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Dark secrets awaken at Hogwarts; a mystery and a test of friendship.

3. Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

The series grows darker; time travel elements and revelations about the past.

4. Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

International tournament, danger, and the return of a great threat.

5. Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Resistance at Hogwarts, political interference, and growing stakes.

6. Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

Secrets of the past and preparations for the final conflict.

7. Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

The epic conclusion: quests, sacrifices, and the end of a saga.

Reading tips

  1. Read the main seven in order for best experience.
  2. Use companion books for world-building after book 3.
  3. Host book-club discussions for deeper themes.
